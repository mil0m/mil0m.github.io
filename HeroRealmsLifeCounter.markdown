**Hero Realms Life Counter** is an iOS app for tracking life totals in the deck-building game Hero Realms.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I end a game?**
You get the prompt to save match data when the life of a player reaches zero.

**Why don't I get the prompt to save match data?**
You have to make sure that both players are named, and are not the default Player1 or Player2.

**Can I change the amount of healing/damage that the different arrows do?**
As of now, no. This is planned in a future update.

**I don't want all the matches of this player to be included in the statistics anymore!**
The easy way to handle that is to remove that player from the player list which will remove all matches including that player.
